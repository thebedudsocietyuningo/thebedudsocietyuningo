What We Do!

Through the past 20 years of continuous efforts, the Bengal Education Development Society has touched thousands of lives, fostering hope, equality, and positive change. We believe change begins with us, and together, we can create a better tomorrow. Whether through volunteering, partnerships, or donations, your support helps us continue our mission to build a brighter, healthier, and more empowered society.

Let’s work together for education, health, environment, and humanity.

Over the last two decades, we have successfully conducted a wide range of programs and initiatives, including:

✅ Education & Scholarships

Running free schools for school dropouts

Organizing the Bengal Talent Search Scholarship Exam to identify and support talented students

Conducting skill development training and workshops for youth and women

✅ Healthcare & Awareness

Free medical camps & charitable clinics

Low-cost pathological tests and eye care services

Blood donation camps and Thalassemia awareness programs

Yoga sessions for physical and mental well-being

✅ Environmental & Social Initiatives

Tree plantation drives and Save Water Movement

Campaigns against plastic pollution and drug abuse

Programs to eradicate superstitions and promote scientific thinking

✅ Women & Community Empowerment

Women empowerment projects through training and self-help initiatives

Promoting peace, harmony, and cultural integration through cultural activities

Extending support during natural disasters with relief and rehabilitation
